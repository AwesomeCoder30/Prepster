Track your pre-med journey with smart progress tracking, automated reminders, and a dynamic dashboard that monitors your classes, clinical hours, and milestones. Your roadmap automatically updates based on your target med schools' prerequisites.

Smart MCAT Planner

Input your target MCAT date and get personalized weekly study goals, curated resource links, Anki deck recommendations, and dynamic study calendars. Our AI adapts your plan based on your progress and keeps you accountable with smart reminders and progress tracking.

About Us

As premed students and aspiring medical professionals, we intimately understand the challenges of this journey. We created this platform to help fellow premeds succeed, especially those who are passionate about medicine but need guidance navigating the complex path to medical school. Our mission is to provide consolidated, actionable resources that make the pre-med journey clearer and more achievable. Our team of successful medical school students and experienced advisors understands that every pre-med journey is unique, which is why we've built an adaptive platform that evolves with your needs and goals.
