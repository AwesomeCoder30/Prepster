Dream School:

Stanford University

Curated 4-Year Pre-Med Journey

1st Year Goals

  • 📋 300 Clinical Hours
    How?

    • Join Stanford's Clinical Immersion Program
    • Shadow doctors at Stanford Health Care

  • 📋 300 Volunteer Hours
    How?

    • Volunteer at the Bing Overseas Studies Program
    • Tutor local high-school students

  • 📋 100 Shadowing Hours
    How?

    • Network through Stanford Pre-Med Club
    • Attend hospital shadowing workshops

  • 📋 200 Research Hours
    How?

    • Apply to labs via Stanford Undergrad Research Database
    • Assist on a faculty-led project

2nd Year Goals

  • 📋 Leadership Roles
    How?

    • Serve as officer in Stanford Pre-Med Club
    • Organize volunteer expeditions

  • 📋 Join a Research Lab
    How?

    • Reach out to professors in Bio-E department
    • Volunteer for ongoing studies

  • 📋 MCAT Prep (light)
    How?

    • Start with AAMC diagnostic exam
    • Join an MCAT study group on campus

3rd Year Goals

  • 📋 Take the MCAT
    How?

    • Register on AAMC portal 6 months out
    • Complete weekly full-length practice exams

  • 📋 Letters of Recommendation
    How?

    • Ask research mentor & professors early
    • Provide them your CV & personal statement draft

  • 📋 Write Personal Statement
    How?

    • Draft over Winter break
    • Get feedback from Stanford writing center

4th Year Goals

  • 📋 Submit Secondaries
    How?

    • Prioritize schools by deadline
    • Reuse core essays where possible

  • 📋 Attend Interviews
    How?

    • Practice with mock MMI sessions
    • Research each school's mission statement

  • 📋 Decision Time
    How?

    • Compare financial aid packages
    • Visit Stanford campus if offered
